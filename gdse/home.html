Welcome to Haridha Sangamam - Let's Clean Together!

Haridha Sangamam is a community-driven initiative aimed at cleaning and beautifying our locality while addressing the challenges and issues faced by individuals. By coming together and working as a team, we can make a tangible difference in our environment and create a cleaner, healthier community for all.


How It Works:

1.Submit Your Problem:

Are you facing a cleanliness-related issue in your locality? Whether it's littering, waste management, or environmental pollution, let us know by submitting your problem on our platform.


2.Volunteer Solutions:

Volunteer groups comprising passionate individuals and organizations will come forward to solve the problems posted on our platform. They will devise innovative solutions and take action to address the issues effectively.


3.Earn Reward Points:

As a volunteer group, for every successfully implemented solution, you'll earn reward points from Haridha Sangamam. These points can be redeemed for discounts on products from our partner companies, including fertilizer and recycling companies.


Our Partners and Sponsors:

Our initiative is made possible thanks to the generous support of our partner companies, including fertilizer and recycling companies. They share our commitment to environmental sustainability and community development, and they offer discounts on their products as rewards for the dedicated efforts of our volunteers.


Join Us in Making a Difference:

Join the Haridha Sangamam community today and be a part of the movement to clean and rejuvenate our locality. Together, we can create a cleaner, greener environment and enjoy rewards from our partner companies as a token of appreciation for your efforts.
